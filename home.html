What We Offer

Everything You Need in One Library

A comprehensive digital library management platform built with modern web technologies.

📖

Smart Book Catalog

Browse, search, and filter hundreds of books by title, author, or category. Real-time availability shown instantly.

📤

Issue & Return System

Admins can issue books to students, track due dates, and process returns — with overdue alerts built in.

Course Material Upload

Teachers upload PDFs, lecture slides, assignments, and lab manuals that students can download anytime.

Notification Board

Admin posts important notices. Students and teachers see them instantly on their dashboards.
