Services

What Our Library Offers

Comprehensive digital services for every member of the Premier University academic community.

Core Services

Everything the Library Provides

01
📖

Book Catalog & Search

Browse our full collection of academic books. Search by title, author, or category. Check real-time availability.

  • Full-text search across titles and authors
  • Category filtering (CSE, EEE, Math…)
  • Real-time availability count
  • PDF download for digital books
02
📤

Book Issue & Return

Admins issue physical books to students and process returns. Full history tracked with due dates and overdue alerts.

  • 14-day issue period by default
  • Overdue detection & alerts
  • Complete issue history log
  • Student dashboard tracking
03
📁

Course Material Upload

Teachers upload PDFs, lecture slides, assignments, and lab manuals for students to download anytime.

  • Support for PDF, DOCX, PPTX, ZIP
  • Access control (all users / teachers only)
  • Course-wise organization
  • Instant download links
04
📢

Notice Board

Admin posts library announcements. Visible instantly on dashboards for all students and teachers.

  • XML-powered notice data
  • Real-time display updates
  • Date-stamped entries
  • Admin can delete notices
05
👤

User Management

Students and teachers can self-register. Admin views all registered accounts with their details and book counts.

  • Self-registration for students & teachers
  • Admin user overview panel
  • Role-based access control
  • Session-based authentication
06
💬

Feedback & Contact

Students and teachers can send messages or feedback to the admin through the integrated contact system.

  • Contact form for students
  • Anonymous feedback option
  • Teacher-to-admin messaging
  • Admin feedback review panel
